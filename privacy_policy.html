Privacy Policy - Unison Clips

Last updated: July 2026

Unison Clips is a personal-use tool operated solely by its owner ("we", "us"). It is not distributed to the public and does not collect data from any third party.

What we access

YouTube: We use the YouTube Data API to search for and download publicly available video content, and to upload finished clips to the owner's own YouTube channel via OAuth. We do not access other users' private YouTube data.

TikTok: We use the TikTok Content Posting API, authorized via OAuth, solely to upload or post video clips to the owner's own authorized TikTok account. We do not access, store, or share any other TikTok user's data.

What we store

We store OAuth tokens (access/refresh tokens) locally on the owner's own device to maintain login sessions. We do not transmit these tokens to any third party. We do not sell, share, or monetize any data.

Data retention

Locally cached tokens and video files remain on the owner's device until manually deleted. No data is retained on any external server operated by us.
